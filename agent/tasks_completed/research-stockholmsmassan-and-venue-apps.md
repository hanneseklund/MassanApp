# Research Stockholmsmassan And Venue Apps

References:
- issue #1: Start project
- https://github.com/hanneseklund/MassanApp/issues/1

Objective:
- Gather the research input required by issue #1 before specification and implementation work starts.

Deliverables:
- Document the basic facts about Stockholmsmassan AB and the venue at `stockholmsmassan.se`.
- Review upcoming events from `https://stockholmsmassan.se/en/calendar/` and collect candidate prototype data sources.
- Research how event venues operate, including trade shows, conferences, annual meetings, shareholder meetings, and political summits.
- Compare those venue needs with smaller arenas such as movie theaters, live theaters, and football stadiums.
- Catalog existing venue and event-app functionality and derive a recommended MassanApp feature list.

Acceptance Criteria:
- Findings are captured in repo documentation or research notes that later tasks can reference.
- The recommended feature list covers the minimum flows explicitly required in issue #1.
